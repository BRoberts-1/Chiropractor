Chiropractic Therapy for Rehabilitation and Personal Injury.

Our clinic focuses on the treatment of injuries sustained in motor vehicle accidents. Our staff is well trained in the treatment of soft tissue, disc, and joint related injures that can occur as a result of a motor vehicle collision.

About Us

Dr. Michael Roberts has been working in personal injury for more than 9 years in Houston,Texas. He has helped countless patients recover from their injuries and get their lives back on track.
If you have been injured, it’s important to act fast and get the right people in your corner. Dr. Roberts has extensive experience in all kinds of musculoskeletal injuries both chronic and acute. Working in rural China, Vietnam, and Thailand, he has seen and treated a variety of conditions including: chronic neck and back pain, severe spinal degeneration, scoliosis, and ankylosing spondylitis.
Dr. Roberts graduated from Texas Chiropractic College in 2008 and has been licensed to practice in Texas since 2009

Our Services...

Chiropractic Therapy

  • Chiropractic Spinal Manipulation or “Chiropractic Adjustments” are a safe and effective treatment for a variety of musculoskeletal issues that cause pain and discomfort.
  • Focuses on disorders of the musculoskeletal system, the peripheral nervous system, and the effects of these disorders on general health.
  • Treats neuromusculoskeletal complaints, including back pain, neck pain, pain in the joints of the arms or legs, and headaches.

Personal Injury
